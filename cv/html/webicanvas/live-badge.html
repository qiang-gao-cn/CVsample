The Summary mode allows user to have a quick overview on various status of Webi without opening them in Full screen view in the Open mode.

A freebie badge layout means you can freely layout and format the content of a badge in anyway you like. Its not advisable to use freebie badge except for development purpose.

Tinky is the most handy and flexible live badge. It has a title bar on top, and a label and value. The title bar indicates the name of the "Group" this Tinky belongs to. Think of Tinky as a breakdown of one UI into tiny square each just showing one item and its value. Different items may belong to the same group. For example, if you have a Weather Tinky, you may have Temperature, Humidity, Direction of wind as three Tinkies.

Po is a vertically layout Live Badge with a top title area and multi-columns of main areas. Each main area can also have a title on top of it. In this example the main area comprises two vertical gauge bars
